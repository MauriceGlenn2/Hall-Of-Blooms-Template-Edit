How it Works

1. The bouquet bar experience starts at $350.
a. 4 hour rental. Additional hours by request for a fee.
b. 20 bouquets with 3-5 stems each.

2. Hall of Blooms sources florals in the color scheme of your choice. You don’t have to go searching for them!

3. We deliver and set up 30 minutes before your scheduled rental time.

4. Party Time! Enjoy creating bouquets with your gals & pals. Take pictures with your blooms & share with us on instagram!

5. Hall of Blooms arrives at the scheduled end time and cleans up the bouquet bar area for you, while leaving no fallen petals behind.
