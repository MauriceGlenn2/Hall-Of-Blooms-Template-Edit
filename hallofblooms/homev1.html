FAQs

Q: Where do you get the flowers?
A: We locally source florals in the color scheme of your choice.

Q: What's included in the rental?
A: Hall of Blooms flower cart, fresh pre-cut florals, flower buckets, bouquet paper, twine or ribbon, scissors, custom chalkboard*

Q: Is there an attendant included in the rental?
A: No. Set up and all materials are included in your rental for your guests to use. We have tutorials available ~here~ if you need any assistance.

Q: Is there a delivery fee?
A: Yes. Please email us for more information.

Q: I’m outside San Antonio, can I still rent?
A: Please email us for more information.
